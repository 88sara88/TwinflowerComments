Carrot Lentil Pasta

This is a simply recipe that can help you use up vegetables siting in your fridge. This recipe calls for shredded carrots and broccoli, but nearly any vegetable will work. Steam or roast your broccoli. In the mean time, cook your carrots or other vegetables in some oil and soy sauce or coco aminos until softened. Throw all of your ingredients together in a bowl and you're done! This is a simple yet satisfying meal.
